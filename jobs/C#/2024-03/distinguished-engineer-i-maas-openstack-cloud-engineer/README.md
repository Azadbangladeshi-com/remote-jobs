 **Position Summary**

GEICO is seeking an experienced OpenStack, Metal as a Service (MaaS), Cloud Engineer to be a part of exciting projects focused on building and optimizing large-scale private cloud infrastructures. The ideal candidate is an expert in designing, building, managing, and scaling OpenStack environments, with a deep understanding of OpenSource technologies and a passion for hardware automation and cloud services. This individual thrives in a collaborative atmosphere and possesses a strategic mindset for advancing cloud infrastructure and delivering innovative solutions.

 **Position Description**

As a Distinguished OpenStack, MaaS, Cloud Engineer in our Hardware Engineering team, you will spearhead the design, development, deployment, and management of our advanced private cloud systems. This role requires an individual who can innovate, propose, and deliver robust technology solutions, leveraging extensive experience in hardware automation, server management, and large-scale system design.   * Contribute high-quality, well-documented code, with a commitment to OpenSource community development practices
  * Collaborate on the strategy and implementation of data protection, disaster recovery, and security protocols across all private cloud infrastructures.
  * Drive continuous improvement by staying updated with emerging technologies and industry best practices, ensuring the security, stability, and scalability of the system architecture
  * Accountable for the quality, usability, and performance of the solutions

  * Utilize programming languages like Python, C# or other object-oriented languages, SQL, and NoSQL databases, Container Orchestration services including Docker and Kubernetes, and a variety of Azure tools and services
  * Consistently share best practices and improve processes within and across teams
  * Mentor junior team members, providing guidance and insight related to cloud infrastructure and best practices in system management
