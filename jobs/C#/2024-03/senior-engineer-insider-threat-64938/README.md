**GEICO** is seeking an experienced **Insider Threat Senior Engineer** with a passion for building and implementing high performance, low-latency platforms, and applications. **Position Description:**

Our Insider Threat Senior Engineer will oversee the design and implementation of comprehensive security strategies to mitigate and manage insider threats. This individual will work closely with our cybersecurity team, other technical teams, and business stakeholders to develop advanced insider threat systems and procedures. The ideal candidate is a thought leader on our cybersecurity team, advising on matters of technical architecture and providing guidance in the development of advanced systems to detect, prevent, and mitigate insider threats.

**Position Responsibilities**

As a Senior Engineer, you will:

  * Lead the design, development, and implementation of a comprehensive insider threat program that integrates technical and non-technical components

  * Collaborate with cybersecurity analysts and engineers to develop and refine insider threat detection capabilities

  * Guide the technical architecture of insider threat systems, ensuring alignment with the organization's security and business goals

  * Work closely with business and IT stakeholders to identify critical assets and potential threat vectors

  * Evaluate and recommend security technologies to improve the organization's insider threat posture

  * Define security controls and metrics to measure the effectiveness of the insider threat program

  * Train and mentor junior staff members, fostering a culture of security awareness within the organization

  * Stay abreast of emerging trends in the cybersecurity landscape and update our strategies accordingly

**Qualifications:**
