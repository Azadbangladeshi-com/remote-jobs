###  **What this job can offer you**

  * A chance to work on creating data models that guide incredibly important commercial initiatives
  * Opportunity to work on extremely cross-functional initiatives (incl. Sales, Marketing, Finance, Data Engineering of course Analytics) to broaden big picture knowledge
  * A chance to set standards for high quality analytics engineering practices within the Revenue Analytics team
  * Vast autonomy around your schedule, influence on tooling and the crafting of technical solutions

### What you bring

  * 4+ years of experience in data/analytics/software engineering (Data Analysts or Scientists with demonstrated analytics engineering skills also welcome)
  * Top-Tier written oral communication skills with the ability to adapt and explain complex technical topics for less technical audiences
  * Experience with SQL, ETL, data modelling, and at least one programming/scripting language (e.g. Python, C++, C#, Scala, etc.)
  * Proficient understanding of software engineering practices, including version control, CI/CD, testing and documentation
  * Working knowledge of dbt as well as a data pipeline orchestration tool
  * A pragmatic mindset with a focus on delivering practical solutions and rolling up the sleeves to make an impact, and a desire to collaborate with Data Engineers, Analysts Business Users
  * Writes and speaks fluent English

### Nice To Have:

  * Startup/Scaleup Experience, AWS Knowledge, Comfort with distributed remote teams, EMEA Location, SalesForce Data Experience, Hands-On familiarity with a BI Tool such as Tableau/Looker
  * It's not required to have experience working remotely, but considered a plus

### Job Responsibilities

  * Design, build, and maintain databases and automated data processes, leveraging Python libraries, Scripting and SQL, with a focus on clarity, consistency, and accuracy of data across various platforms and systems
  * Merging, Cleaning Aggregating data from different tools platforms (e.g. Funnel.io, SalesForce, HubSpot, Ad Platforms such as Meta) to create an end-to-end unified picture of Sales, Marketing Revenue Analytics
  * Building Maintaining internal tooling and data aggregations for effective reporting visualizing of KPIs in dashboards, particularly around time-series metrics
  * Liaising productively with Data Analysts and business teams to ensure that data products solutions built are not only technically sound but highly effective and widely useful
  * Bring polished data to Data Analysts and eventually to decision-makers, building pipelines, automations, aggregations and transformations that enable useful insights into Sales, Marketing Revenue Performance
  * Collaborate closely with our data engineering and analytics teams to ensure data reliability, scalability, and accuracy
