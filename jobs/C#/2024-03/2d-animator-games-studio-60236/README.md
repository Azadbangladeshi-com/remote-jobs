Boss Fight is looking for a talented Animator to join our team of artists. In this role, you will work closely with the rest of the animation team to create 2d animations in Spine for our characters, environments, and other various game objects/FX using traditional animation principles.

### Essential Functions/Responsibilities

  * Produce high-quality 2D animations for game objects, characters, environments, and VFX
  * Animate using knowledge of the traditional animation principles
  * Assist with the visual development process by creating aspirational animations 
  * Maintain current and working knowledge of trends and best practices for 2D animations in mobile games 
  * Exemplify reliability, accountability, and professionalism in all work-related interactions 

### Skills and Abilities

  * Self-motivated and driven to create top-quality games
  * Communicates effectively both written and verbal
  * Works well in a collaborative, deadline-focused environment
  * Highly organized and can help others organize 
  * High attention to detail 
  * Able to manage multiple tasks and be self-directed 
  * Positive attitude and ability to receive and provide objective and constructive feedback
  * A balanced mix of visual aesthetic and tech-art/programming skills

### Education and Experience

  * 3 years of experience in a similar role 
  * Expertise in Photoshop and Spine and After Effects 
  * Experience working with outsource houses
  * Familiarity with Unity, C#, and Maya scripting including Mel scripts, Python, PyMel, and/or the Maya API
