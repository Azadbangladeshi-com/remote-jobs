 **About the team**

 **Are you passionate about working with secure applications? Do you thrive in collaborative environments where you can make a real impact?**

We are looking for someone committed to safeguarding the organization and eager to take on the challenges of this role. ### What You'll Be Doing:

*  **Designing, development, and maintenance** of security automations in various domains like threat intelligence and application security, among others. 
* **Automation Development:** Take the lead in designing and maintaining security automations, increasing the efficiency of diverse tasks related to threat intelligence collection, and application security.
*  **Real-time Threat Analysis:** Develop an automated system to collect and analyze threat data from diverse sources, ensuring fast and informed decisions.
*  **Seamless Integration:** Act as a unifying force, working alongside our threat intelligence and application security engineers to create effective security automations that enhance their performance.
*  **Metric Development:** Spearhead the creation and maintenance of KPIs and metrics, ensuring that our security practices remain aligned with our goals and any potential threats can be promptly addressed.

  

###  What You Need To Succeed:

* Proficiency in coding languages, preferably JavaScript/Typescript and hands-on experience with security automation tools.
* A good grasp of threat intelligence, application security and general security operations.
* Analytical skills to identify potential weaknesses and suggest improvements.
* Adept at working in cross-functional teams.
* Excellent communication skills in English.

  

 **Bonus points if you have:**

* Experience with platforms offering cybersecurity information.
* Mastery of cloud security concepts and automation tools.
* Specialization in secure coding practices.

  

We are not expecting candidates to have expertise across all of these areas. We will gladly help you bridge the gaps.
