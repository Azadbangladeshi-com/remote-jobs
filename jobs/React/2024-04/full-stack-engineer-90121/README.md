**How will you make an impact in this role?**

The successful candidate will be responsible for the design, development, and implementation of engineering solutions to support customer journeys at scale. This role involves collaborating closely with SRE Engineers to build a highly resilient and scalable platform. This role will be critical in optimizing our systems for performance and reliability, enabling the delivery of key frameworks to back CCPs and card members. The ideal candidate will have a passion for automating operations, a keen eye for identifying and resolving system bottlenecks, and a dedication to continuous learning in the constantly evolving field of SRE.

**Primary Responsibilities:**

  * Design, deploy and maintain highly scalable and reliable platform to support several customer journeys at scale.
  * Drive initiatives to ensure that the platform which supports customer journeys is highly available, scalable and resilient.
  * Develop automation scripts and tools to streamline deployment, monitoring, and operational processes, reducing manual intervention and improving system resilience.
  * Implement / improve end-to-end observability for customer journey components to gain insights into system performance and identify areas for improvement.
  * Cultivate a highly collaborative, inclusive, strong cohesive and productive team passionate about accomplishing targets in timely manner in line with overall organization strategy and goals.

**Minimum Qualifications:**

  * Bachelor's degree 
  * 5+ years of software engineering experience covering full stack solution development and delivery.
  * Experience with Golang, Java, ReactJS, and Kubernetes
