### Job Description

### What you get to do in this role:

  * This is a writer-manager position divided roughly 50/50.
  * Manage a close-knit team of four writers who document ServiceNow seismic components, which are the building blocks of ServiceNow user interfaces.
  * Write about components using Figma as your authoring tool.
  * Work across the entire organization handling all Usage Guidelines documentation.
  * Create and lead innovative projects to improve the usability and adoption of seismic components.
  * Work closely with other writing teams that use seismic components.
  * Inspire and support direct reports at all career levels.
  * Work with a program manager to weigh and distribute workloads.
  * Collaborate with other content-producing teams to share knowledge and avoid duplicating efforts. 
  * Check in regularly with development team managers to get feedback and, if necessary, help course correct to get deliverables back on track. 
  * Meet one-on-one with each direct report on a weekly or bi-weekly basis. 
  * Conduct quarterly performance-related conversations with team members, provide feedback about areas of improvement, and recognize outstanding efforts. 
  * Recruit excellent team members who are not only solid information developers, but who work well with the rest of the team. 
  * When required, support the team in the development of product documentation for current and future releases of our product. 
  * Review content on a regular basis, provide feedback on progress, adherence to standards, and overall completeness.

### Qualifications

### To be successful in this role:

### Preferred Qualifications:

  * Background in Figma and DITA. 
  * Have a growth mindset.
  * A strong, positive communicator who can relate large-picture initiatives and detailed information to direct reports and upper management.
  * High level of creativity, flexibility, and dependability, and the ability to react quickly in a fast-paced environment. 
  * Knowledge of structured task-based writing. 
  * Proven experience relating to different levels of the organization and working effectively with peers.

  * BS/BA in Technical Communication, English, Computer Science, related field, or equivalent experience, with 5+ years of experience writing software documentation for a technical audience.
  * 3+ years of technical documentation management experience in a large software organization.
