### Position Overview:

The Deployment team at Zscaler helps customers properly deploy their Zscaler solution. A Professional Services Consultant at Zscaler is a senior technical individual contributor, traveling to customer sites to assist in proper design and deployment.  
  
In this role, you will be a key field resource, working hand-in-hand with the deployment team, partners and customers, to provide deployment advisory services to ensure proper design, configuration, resiliency, and overall best practices are followed.

  * Assist deployment customers with testing or troubleshooting. Escalate to support as required
  * Reproduce customer issues to verify problems and document steps to replicate for Support, Engineering and Operations teams
