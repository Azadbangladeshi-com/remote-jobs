###  **Job Description**

As a Distinguished Architect at ServiceNow, you will be a leader with a track record of architecting and incorporating Intelligence into products. Your expertise will be instrumental in driving our AI initiatives to new heights, with a focus on cutting-edge technologies, including deep learning, large language models (LLMs), natural language processing and conversational assistants.

**What you get to do in this role:**

  * Collaborate with Engineering and Product: 

Work with engineering and product on incorporating the AI technologies into the ServiceNow roadmap. You will be expected to innovate and find new and better ways to replace complex frameworks with powerful AI technologies that are scalable and require far less maintenance.

  * Build elegant architectures: 

You will help build elegant architectures that are easy to understand, maintain and enhance over a period.

  * Architect reusable components: 

You will build frameworks for reusable components that can be leveraged across multiple teams. For example, you may build a framework for developing conversational assistants for the finance industry that can leverage data from all our customers with their permission.

  * Influence Leadership: 

Champion AI strategies, technological advancements, and industry trends to ServiceNow's leaders shaping the direction of AI-driven initiatives. Influence peers and stakeholders in driving technical, product and business direction.

Perform competitive analysis:

You will conduct a thorough competitive analysis of various AI products and technologies.

  * Thought Leadership: 

Share your expertise and insights at prestigious AI and technology conferences, elevating ServiceNow's brand and positioning us as a trailblazer in the AI industry. You will publish blogs, papers and patents.
