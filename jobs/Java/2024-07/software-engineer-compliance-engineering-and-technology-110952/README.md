###  **Job Description**

The Cash Compliance Engineering team builds high-scale analysis and visualizations systems in order to find and stop criminal activity and other bad behavior in billions of dollars of person to person, merchant, and Bitcoin transactions across the globe. We design our products and ship code with a customer-centric focus. Humans are at the center of everything we do, whether they are Cash customers or members of our internal operations teams. Uncovering and preventing activities like money laundering, human trafficking, and all manner of criminal fraud before they impact our users and upend their lives is a part of Cash App's mission to bring economic empowerment to all.

You Will:

  * Develop and support our tools as products, with a focus on elegant solutions, usability, and our customer needs.

  * Write and deploy code across all of Cash App's ever-evolving architecture.

  * Integrate machine learning models (both real time and offline) to our systems for ever evolving intelligence on possible bad behavior.

  * Work with Cash App feature teams and support new product launches by building tooling to expose misuse by bad actors.

  * Contribute to our own platform of services and data pipelines, architected for scale, and designed to anticipate the future.
