###  **Job Description**

As the Principal SAP BASIS Administrator, you will provide leadership of the future design and implementation of SAP S/4 on SAP RISE for NBCUniversal. This is a key role within the Corporate Technology organization providing planning, design, leadership, and architectural design for future SAP solutions to be used by NBCUniversal

The position will lead and direct a core group of SAP BASIS Engineers. This is a global role supporting NBCUniversal business worldwide.

Responsibilities include:

  * Provide leadership of daily operations for SAP BASIS activities, including oversight and accountability for relevant global incident/event/request queues, advanced people management of direct and indirect reports within the team. Including all PMP processes.
  * Provide oversight and governance for projects, execute project plans, provide project estimates and landscape recommendations.
  * Work in a fast-paced environment and cooperate with various project teams, including production support teams located off premise
