###  **Job Description**

As a Data Engineer on dentsu's Product Team, you will help us improve the data aspects of our platforms, including the set of analytics and reporting applications it drives. We are looking for Engineers who not only want to build platforms, but also the tooling and systems that allow us to test and operate them. You can expect to be hands-on in supporting and debugging our systems, and you will also need to understand how our systems are used to ensure we build what the business needs. This is a remote position, reporting to Senior Data Engineer.

 **RESPONSIBILITIES**

  * You will Build, Deliver, and Operate Our Platforms
  * You will work as part of a team to implement solutions that best meet our users' needs and will be easy to operate
  * You will use technology and frameworks and ensure work fits well into its application/system
  * You will operate and monitor our applications, data assets, and systems and investigating issues that arise
  * You will communicate with Engineering Team members to facilitate implementation of your work
  * You will present materials that help improve and inform the team
  * You will work with fellow engineers to cover knowledge gaps and identify solutions to problems

###  **Qualifications**

REQUIRED

  * BS degree in relevant technical field (e.g. Computer Science/Engineering) or equivalent experience
  * 3+ years of experience in data platform development; demonstrate successful design, build, rollout and ongoing delivery of business-critical data assets, applications, and pipelines starting from raw business requirements.
  * Non-trivial experience with SQL, data modeling, and RDBMS databases such as Postgres, Microsoft SQL Server, MySQL or Oracle
  * Experience with modern data storage, processing/orchestration, and querying tools such as
  * Snowflake
  * AWS: IAM, S3, EC2, SNS, SQS, SES, Athena, Redshift, Lambda, Glue, Secrets Manager
  * Apache Airflow (MWAA a plus)
  * Apache Spark and Hive
  * Azure: Data Factories, Storage Accounts, Bastions, Key Vault
  * Experience with programming languages like Python, Java, JavaScript, Go, or C#
  * Knowledge of engineering practices like iterative releases, test-driven development, continuous integration, and the use of version control systems (Git/GitHub is our primary system, but experience with others would be helpful too).
  * Experience working with semi-structured data in JSON format
  * Ability to translate business questions and needs into technical projects and specifications
