We are hiring a **Senior Machine Learning Engineer** to our **Data Solutions** team in **Brazil!**

The Data department at Wellhub contributes to our data democratization mission. We are responsible for empowering every team by providing a scalable and reliable Data and ML platform to provide an efficient journey for every Data Practitioner and help elevate business outcomes from our data.

  * You have created robust and scalable Data and Machine Learning architectures and frameworks;
