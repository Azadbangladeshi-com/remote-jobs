We are seeking an experienced Senior QA Engineer to take responsibility for designing, developing, and executing software tests to ensure that our in-house Content Management System (CMS) meets business and product requirements.

As the Senior QA Engineer for the Xumo Software Engineering Team, you will play a pivotal role in the testing and quality assurance of our CMS. This position requires a strong background in QA and testing methodologies, as you will be responsible for thoroughly testing the frontend application, backend functionality (including API testing and data verification), and the overall infrastructure to ensure a seamless and reliable user experience for millions of viewers accessing our content via various streaming devices and internal/external operators through our CMS. To excel in this role, you must consistently provide clear and effective technical solutions that align with the business requirements set by the product and operations teams.
