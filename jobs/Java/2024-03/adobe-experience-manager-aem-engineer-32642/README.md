**Job Summary:** Rackspace is looking for a **AEM Engineer** to join our team. Rackspace AEM Engineers are an extension of our Fanatical Support team and are responsible for providing deep application support and advisory services for the AEM platform and associated technologies. AEM Engineers are expected to work closely with Rackers, customers, and systems integrators to provide a seamless and customized design, architecture, and support experience. Engineers engage to resolve customer issues, manage ongoing technical projects, and support our AEM Architects, who act as thought leaders by shaping and defining the offering.

The Engineer will support and maintain customer AEM configurations through all phases of the customer lifecycle including initial design, implementation, performance tuning, and security hardening. In addition, this role will assist and support the AEM Architect in developing automation around the AEM platform to provide scalable and repeatable solutions for application installation and customer deployments. Acting as a partner to our customers and internal teams, the AEM Engineer is responsible for creating customer loyalty through extensive knowledge of application and infrastructure performance tuning, and by working with internal teams to deliver a comprehensive, consistent, and Fanatical customer experience.

The AEM Engineer takes on the additional responsibilities of representing Rackspace in product meetings and to third-party vendors, and in mentoring and developing more junior team members. The AEM Engineer will often be tasked with resolving complex customer issues, facilitating OS-specific meetings and training sessions for knowledge sharing, managing long-running projects for internal and external customers, and owning the technical development of the Rackspace Application Services (RAS) team, specific to AEM product services.
