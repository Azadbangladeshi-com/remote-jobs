 _ **General Summary:**_

# Responsibilities

  * Design, develop, and maintain data pipelines that ingest, transform, and integrate data from disparate sources such as ITSM, TBM, PPM, CMDB, and EA systems.
  * Build and optimize data models that support business intelligence and reporting needs for the Business of IT.
  * Create and deliver reports and dashboards that provide actionable insights into the performance and efficiency of our IT organization.
  * Collaborate with business stakeholders, IT leaders, and data analysts to understand and define data requirements, metrics, and KPIs.
  * Ensure data quality, accuracy, and consistency across data sources and reports.
  * Research and evaluate new data sources, tools, and technologies to enhance our data model and analytics capabilities.
  * Document and communicate data engineering and analytics processes, methodologies, and best practices.

# Qualifications

  * Bachelor's degree in a technical field such as computer science, engineering, mathematics, statistics, or related field.  
At least 5 years of experience in data engineering and data analytics, preferably in the IT domain.

  * Proficient in SQL and Python, and familiar with other programming languages such as R, Java, or Scala.
  * Experience with data warehouse and data lake architectures, data modeling techniques, and tools.
  * Experience with cloud-based data platforms and services such as AWS and Azure, and data pipeline tools such as Airflow, Spark, or Kafka.
  * Experience with business intelligence and reporting tools such as Power BI or Tableau.
  * Knowledge of business intelligence and analytics concepts and methodologies, such as data quality, data governance, data visualization, and data storytelling.
  * Knowledge of IT business processes and functions, such as ITSM, ITFM, PPM, CMDB, and EA.
  * Strong analytical and problem-solving skills, and attention to detail.
  * Familiarity with agile development methodologies and CICD pipelines, and ability to automate and monitor the deployment of data solutions through test, stage, to production environments.
  * Excellent communication and collaboration skills, and ability to work in a fast-paced and dynamic environment.
