### Job Description

As team lead of our new Data Integration and Management Team you will play a key role in Scalable's data journey contributing with excellent software engineering and team management skills. The team is focusing on data projects and data management related software (backend & frontend) solutions on a tech stack mainly consisting of Java/ Kotlin/ javascript/ React. On the one hand, the team is responsible for bringing data products (e.g. machine learning models) to production by developing suitable backend / frontend solutions. Furthermore, the development of central and automated data management solutions (like deletions of personal data) in the Engineering Department belongs to the team's core mission.
