ServiceNow is looking for a Senior Staff Software Engineer. This senior engineer will join a team of Engineers, Product Managers, and Solution Architects within the IAM Development Team.

This position will include hands-on design/architecture, engineering and development of IAM solutions using the Sailpoint IIQ platform. Operating within DevOps and Agile frameworks as part of the IAM Product Management Model.

_**What you get to do in this role:**_

  * Play a pivotal role in the design/architecture, development, and implementation of best-in-class Identity and Access Management (IAM) solutions using Sailpoint IIQ 

  * Work within, and across, Agile teams to enable a more secure Enterprise where the right people have access to the right resources at the right time 

  * Collaborate with other engineering teams to design appropriate identity and access management solutions to ensure seamless identity integration 

  * Design, develop, and implement technical solutions as part of our Identity and Access Management strategy to meet risk and compliance regulations 

  * Partner with cross-functional teams to assess and address security risks across the ServiceNow landscape 

  * Engage with the business users at different levels to understand Application Owner business needs, analyze, document, evaluate and prioritize requirements and make recommendations on the solution and implementation 

  * Provide functional leadership during technology enhancements and upgrades implementations and work successfully as a liaison with various internal business technical partners 

  * Deploy solutions through Continuous Improvement/Continuous Development strategies 

  * Partner with the support organization to provide training, support and technical assistance to operations team and end users. 

  * Be a strong team player that works well horizontally and vertically with others of varying skill levels and experience
