## **The Opportunity**

Flock Safety is looking to add a Staff Camera Software Engineer to our rapidly growing Imaging Team within our Device Engineering organization. Flock Safety is continuously building more and more complex and efficient camera systems to ensure continuous development in our capture and detection quality. We are also introducing new products with added features. This role strengthens our capability to implement advanced camera features and optimize the related camera control algorithm.

This position is on-site in Tampere, Finland.

## **The Skillset**

**We're looking for people with:**

  * Bachelors/Master of Science in Computer Engineering, Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, or related field with 5+ years experience
  * Experience on developing new camera features within the Qualcomm/Android camera pipeline (Chi, Camx)
  * Extensive experience on with camera software/hardware image signal processing and algorithms
  * Experience on auto exposure (AE) and auto focus (AF) development and optimizations on Android platform
  * Android OS and middleware development experience (C++)
  * Low-level firmware software development experience (for example external MCUs)
  * Familiarity with source control solutions such as (git/Github/Gerrit)
  * Familiarity with modern software development practices and tools (DevOps)
  * Are comfortable working cross-functionally across multiple projects
  * Possess effective verbal and written communication skills
  * Have humility, curiosity, and a passion for sharing knowledge

**The ideal candidate will have:**

  * Android OS and application development experience (Java/Kotlin)
  * Familiarity with Android Camera2 and MediaCodec APIs
  * Ability to develop by using test-driven development
  * Experience mentoring members of the team
