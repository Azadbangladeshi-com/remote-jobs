### Responsibilities

* BI Solution Development: Design, build, and deploy business intelligence (BI) reports, dashboards, and visualizations using tools like Power BI, Tableau, or similar platforms.
* Data Integration: Collaborate with data engineers and other IT team members to ensure data is appropriately sourced, transformed, and loaded into BI solutions.
* Database Management: Manage data models and databases (e.g., GCP,SQL Server, Oracle, or other relational and non-relational databases), ensuring data integrity, accuracy, and performance.
* Data Analysis: Analyze and understand business requirements to translate them into functional BI reports that address business needs.
* Collaboration: Work closely with cross-functional teams including business analysts, data scientists, and department leads to gather requirements and deliver solutions.
* Documentation & Support: Create and maintain technical documentation, including system design, process flow diagrams, and user manuals. Provide support and training to end-users as needed.

* Requires a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, MIS, or Business Administration.

* Experience with cloud platforms (e.g., AWS, Azure) is a plus.
* Knowledge of programming languages like Python or R is an advantage.
* Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
