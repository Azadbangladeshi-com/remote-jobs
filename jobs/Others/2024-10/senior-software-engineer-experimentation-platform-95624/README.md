**About the Experimentation Platform team:**

The Experimentation Platform team at Liftoff is focused on improving Accelerate's performance by increasing the rate and rigor of experiments through better statistics, methodologies, data, and tools. In this role you would maintain AB testing infrastructure, refining core metrics, developing testing protocols, and implementing system improvements to enhance experimentation velocity.

**Responsibilities:**

  * Work with an experienced team of Machine Learning, Software, and Infrastructure Engineers that are building the next generation ML platform for Liftoff.
  * Design, engineer & implement reliable, scalable, and cost-efficient systems to support our next generation ML Platform, with a focus on experimentation services and tooling.
  * Design and optimize systems for A/B testing and other experimental frameworks. 
    * Lead the development and maintenance of our experimentation platform to support high-volume A/B testing.
  * Enhance the platform to run more experiments simultaneously and improve the speed and accuracy of result analysis.
  * Deliver business results by improving the pace of R&D performance releases.
  * Utilize vendor-based products (AWS, Tableau, Neptune, etc.), open-source technologies (PyTorch, Trino, PySpark, etc.) and in-house tooling.

**Requirements:**

  * Bachelors in Computer Science or similar field, or equivalent work experience
  * 5+ years of experience in software development, with some experience in machine learning and data analysis.
  * Very strong coding ability in one of the following languages: Go, Python or Clojure 
  * Strong core CS fundamentals (data structures, algorithms, architecting systems)
