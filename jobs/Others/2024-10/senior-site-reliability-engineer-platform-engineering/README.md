**THE OPPORTUNITY**

We are hiring a **Senior Site Reliability Engineer** to our **Platform Engineering team** in **Brazil**!

Platform Engineering builds tooling and automation to eliminate operational processes or drive them to as near zero as possible. We want to enable the most reliable, real-time logistics platform possible. We want the Wellhub technology stack to be completely autonomous but resilient and reliable.

We are continuously evaluating infrastructure-related operational processes, looking for opportunities to make it as frictionless as possible, and delivering intuitive and reliable tools for other teams to use in their daily activities. We strive to positively impact a large number of engineers on our organization throughout our deliveries.

Our teams are located in Brazil and Portugal, and they are composed of software and systems engineers working in small teams focused on Cloud, Runtime, Observability and Productivity engineering. Together we develop and manage a huge multi-services cloud native platform on top of Kubernetes on AWS.
