### Responsibilities

* Perform cost estimating and evaluation for assigned projects. 
* Conduct requirements analysis, risk assessments, and technology evaluations as needed by the Government. 
* Monitor and track day-to-day project status, including on-site walkthroughs and documentation of progress. 
* Report project development and progress metrics to the assigned Project Manager (PM). 
* Identify and analyze equipment and facilities issues, providing recommendations for solutions. 
* Participate in meetings to support the assigned PM in managing equipment and facilities projects. 

  

### Qualifications

* Secret Security Clearance 
* A Bachelor's degree from an ABET accredited program in Industrial, Mechanical, or Electrical engineering from a college or university preferred. 
* A minimum total of ten years experience in production, manufacturing, facilities, or supply chain management within the DoD or Automotive Industries. 
* Experience supporting the US Army or DoD is preferred including working with/on tracked or wheeled ground combat vehicles in military or civilian capacity (operator, maintainer, logistics, acquisition, or production).
