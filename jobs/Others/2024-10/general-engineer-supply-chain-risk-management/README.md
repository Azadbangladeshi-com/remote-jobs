* Perform electrical design reviews, trade-space recommendations, and technology assessments. 
* Conduct system integration, failure analysis, and design reviews for lifecycle cost reductions. 
* Provide subject matter expertise on vehicle electronics and electrical systems, including reviews, evaluations, and technical input.
