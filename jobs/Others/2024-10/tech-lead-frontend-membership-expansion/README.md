**THE OPPORTUNITY**

We are hiring a **Tech Lead Frontend** for the **Membership Expansion (CODE)** team in **Portugal!**

CODE is an acronym for Consumer Digital Experience, which means we're in charge of the experience and services that will guide people in their well-being journey. From knowing about Wellhub, and getting access to exclusive benefits, to using our vast partner's network, it is in the end-user experience. CODE teams are responsible for developing and maintaining Wellhub's App, frontend, and backend services, and Design System and work closely with product, UX, and data teams to create an excellent experience for the users of our platform.

The Membership Expansion team's main goal is to find opportunities to increase the membership rate inside Wellhub, being responsible for the end-user family member management, eligibility switch, and other journeys.  
The main scope of this position is being responsible for the definition of the architecture and new features related to increase the membership rate inside Wellhub, provide support to the product team during the definition of the roadmap, and manage the engineering team.
