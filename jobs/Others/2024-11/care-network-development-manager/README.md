## Description

The Care Network Development Manager reports to and works with the Director of Provider Partnerships within our Chief Health Officer organization, to implement a cohesive business development strategy to develop and implement our external clinical relationships.

  

You will support growth by strengthening provider partnerships and network development through strategic evaluation, business development, and partner engagement. Responsibilities include expanding the provider network, collaborating with leadership to create scalable partnership strategies and frameworks. You will be working with care, clinical, and product teams to enhance partner use and guide patient care innovations. Additionally, you will manage partner needs, oversee contractual and operational support, and advocate for provider communities within Included Health to foster growth-oriented relationships.
