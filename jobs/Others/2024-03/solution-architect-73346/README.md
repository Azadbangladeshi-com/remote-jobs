### Job Description

You will articulate and lead design and implementation for Merkle Promotions and Loyalty Marketing Technology integration architectures within client ecosystems. You will work with teams to develop an understanding of the functional/non-functional requirements of technical implementations to meet client objectives, the platform deliverables necessary to achieve those objectives, and capabilities to deliver. The solution architect will follow the evolution of marketing technology ecosystems and drive implementations into the platform. You will also work with the Product team to communicate technical trends in market to influence the future of Merkle internal technology.

The architectures they develop are based on the tactical needs of our Delivery teams and strategic needs aligned with client vision, metrics, and sustainability. The Solution Architect creates the foundation for success on top of Merkle native technology and Loyalty experience. Reporting to the Solutions Architect Lead. Remote (EST/CST).

### Responsibilities:

  * Design and lead client implementation of application architecture and technical delivery for experience-driven, multi-channel, deployed on our cloud-based SAAS platform
  * Translate business requirements into technical implementation and documentation across platforms
  * Consult with teams to develop Loyalty solutions, identify opportunities to modify/mature solution architecture to accommodate changes across multiple technical projects and implementations
  * Participate in training on Merkle alliance/partner platforms and develop POC reference implementations for integration into partner platforms
  * Establish/document the technical strategy, goals, and capabilities appropriate to mitigate unknowns in complex solution ecosystems
  * Maintain an advanced level of platform and product knowledge
  * Perform technical architecture, system analysis, and requirements analysis to determine project requirements to ensure projects are scoped, architected, tested, delivered on time, and sustainable

### Qualifications

### Required Qualifications:

  * BA/BS degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, Engineering or related tech field
  * 5+ years' experience delivering software development projects
  * Demonstrated experience in technical leadership for enterprise software development projects
  * Experience integrating synchronous and asynchronous APIs, data feeds, and flat files
  * Knowledgeable in usage and development of UML based designs, experience modeling dynamic software behavior
  * Experience developing in JavaScript, Java, Ruby, or Perl
  * Experience in SQL data modeling, data loading, and data analysis; cloud based Big Data Solution
  * 3+ years of experience of hands-on delivery and implementation on high transaction SaaS Loyalty platforms
  * Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP)
  * Experience sizing and architecting Amazon Web Services, SaaS delivered Solutions
