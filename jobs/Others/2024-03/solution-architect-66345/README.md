### Job Description

The Solution Architect will be responsible for articulating and driving the design and implementation for Merkle Promotions and Loyalty Marketing Technology integration architectures within client ecosystems. They will work closely with the Client Relationship, Sales Engineering, Strategy, and Business Analysis teams to develop a deep understanding of the functional and non-functional requirements of technical implementations to meet client objectives, the platform deliverables necessary to achieve those objectives, and the Merkle capabilities to deliver. The position requires excellent communication skills and the ability to translate business requirements into technical implementation and documentation across platforms. The solution architect will follow the evolution of marketing technology ecosystems and drive novel implementations into the platform as core capabilities. They will also work with the Product team to communicate technical trends in market to influence the future of
Merkle internal technology.

The Solution Architect is a leader, not a gatekeeper. The architectures they develop are based on the tactical needs of our Delivery teams and strategic needs aligned with client vision, metrics, and sustainability. Our Delivery teams are awesome and motivated towards success based on client goals and metrics. The Solution Architect creates the foundation for success on top of Merkle native technology and Loyalty experience.

### Responsibilities:

  * Design and lead client facing implementation of application architecture and technical delivery for experience-driven, multi-channel, enterprise customers deployed on our cloud based SAAS platform.
  * Consult with solution architects, infrastructure management, and Delivery teams to develop Loyalty solutions, identify opportunities to modify and mature solution architecture to accommodate changes across multiple technical projects and implementations
  * Build alignment with Product, Solution Architecture, Client Relationship, Strategy, and Delivery teams on features necessary to support client technical deliverables and success metrics.
  * Participate in training on Merkle alliance/partner platforms and develop POC reference implementations for integration into partner platforms.
  * Establish and document the technical strategy, goals, and capabilities appropriate to mitigate unknowns in complex solution ecosystems by collaborating with technical resources from client teams.
  * Maintain an advanced level of platform and product knowledge
  * Perform technical architecture, system analysis, and requirements analysis to determine project requirements to ensure projects are clearly scoped, architected, tested, delivered on time, and sustainable.
