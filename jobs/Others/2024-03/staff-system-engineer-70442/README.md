### Job Description

 **What you get to do in this role:**

  * Compile, deliver, and promote end-to-end solution architecture roadmaps that guide product and service evolution.
  * Collaborate with development, infrastructure, test, and production support teams to ensure proper implementation of solutions.
  * Support built systems, web services, and automation tools that are thoroughly specified, documented, and testable.
  * Drive monitoring and automation initiatives.
  * Excellent collaboration and communication skills.
  * Strong problem-solving, leadership, time management, and critical thinking skills.
  * Investigate and discover on your own, being self-driven and accountable.
  * Participate in a 24/7 global on-call rotation for production support.

### Qualifications

 **To be successful in this role you have:**

  * Deep knowledge of Linux/RHEL, databases and TCP/IP networks.
  * An in-depth understanding of the technology associated with operating a service or platform in the public or private cloud, including data centers, systems, networks, web applications, and relational databases.
  * 5+ years experience building and maintaining Linux-based systems and automated tooling such as (but not limited to) Python, Bash, JavaScript, Puppet and Ansible.
  * 5+ years experience working on one (or more) Relational Database technologies: MariaDB, Oracle or PostgreSQL.
  * Familiarity with networking and storage concepts such as routing, IP networking, DNS, load-balancers, block and file storage, etc.
  * Is comfortable designing, authoring, testing, and debugging code in a popular systems languages: Bash, Python, Ruby, Perl.

  * Valid authorization to work in Japan.
  * Business level fluency in both English and Japanese languages.
