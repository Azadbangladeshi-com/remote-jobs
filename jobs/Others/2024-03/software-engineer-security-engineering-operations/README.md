### Job Description

The Security Engineering & Operations team (SecEngOps) within Infrastructure Security help other teams across InfoSec, Compliance and Partner Product teams to protect Block systems, users & customers; encouraging our employees to behave securely; identifying security threats; and responding to incidents. Our customers expect us to protect their data and assets. As Block grows and expands, cyber security will play an increasingly important role in preventing disruption and protecting our reputation. The team is composed of software engineers, who are not only focused and passionate about designing, implementing and maintaining software and services but also monitoring and improving their operational posture - SLA / SLO for uptime, service reliability, performance and deployment automation.

You will:

  * Use your creativity and expertise to build scalable cloud-native data pipelines and detection systems that delight 
  * Collaborate as a team to iterate towards our best - such as reviewing code changes, suggesting process improvements, and offering design ideas 
  * Participate in our on call rotation to ensure our platform and customers have support when they need it the most

### Qualifications

You have:

  * 2+ years of industry experience as a programer, developer, SWE, or similar job roles.
  * A strong interest in security and growing your career in the direction of InfoSec (prior experience in InfoSec not required).
  * Comfortable working with outside teams to address problems and determine priorities.
  * Experience using cloud-native technologies for data processing.
  * Experience working in or with SRE or security detection and response teams.
