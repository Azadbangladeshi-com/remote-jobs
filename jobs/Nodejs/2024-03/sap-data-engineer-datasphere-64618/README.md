Job Description

Embark in a transformational journey with NBCUniversal as the company implements the data mesh and self-service data integration with SAP Datasphere. Your mission? Facilitating a seamless transition during our SAP S/4 transformation by ensuring efficient migration of both Master Data and transactional data. In this role, you will have the opportunity to work with a variety of technologies such as, native HANA modeling, HANA Smart Data Integration, SAP SLT for replication, ABAP CDS View development for analytics, SAP Analytics Cloud, SAP Datasphere and SAP BW4/HANA. Join us!

  * As an SAP Data Engineer, you will be responsible for gathering requirements, design, and build of SAP Datasphere landscape.
  * Integrate SAP Datasphere with SAP BW/4 HANA and BW bridge development to support various data and analytics initiatives.
  * Develop, deliver, and support analytic solutions in alignment with business requirements using SAC, HANA, HANA Cloud, and Datasphere.
  * Utilize best practice architecture and design principles to create analytical data models.
  * Understand customer demands, challenge requirements, and translate them into effective analytical solutions.
  * Strengthen the team by contributing your development, analytical solution, and collaboration skills.
  * Identify, challenge, and resolve existing reporting content issues through innovative ideas that enhance business value.
  * Demonstrate a high level of motivation and enthusiasm in improving solutions and processes.
  * Contribute to data management initiatives to ensure data integrity, quality, and common definitions.
  * Build secure and compliant cloud analytic solutions adhering to best practices in data security and privacy.
  * Support management reporting efforts from a data perspective to drive key business decisions.
  * Collaborate with peers in the Corporate Technology team (BTP/Security/System Engineering) to apply best practices.
  * Reviewing and updating support & operations documentation where required
  * Participating in production support role during key activity periods when required month-end, quarter-end, year-end etc.
  * Ensuring the consistency of master data between MDG and related systems
  * Reviewing & improving existing master data interfaces to eliminate master data issues in SAP ECC, SAP S/4, BW, BPC and any downstream systems.
  * Liaising with other Product teams to ensure timely resolution of all issues and to promote good working relationships.
