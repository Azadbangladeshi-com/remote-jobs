**Job Description Summary:**

Identity Engineering team is responsible for providing Identity Access Management capabilities for PayPal users and partners. We design, develop, enhance services that get called across every interaction between PayPal and its users and partners. Our solutions need to scale across billions of such interactions, working with petabyte plus data, integrating with various other APIs in real time and yet providing high availability and configurability to the business. To achieve this, we are always looking at advances in technology and science and tying those back to business needs.  
  
The Identity Experiences engineering team is seeking a Software Engineer passionate about designing topnotch experiences while utilizing latest web technologies. In this role, you will work with a team of engineers and build software for both PayPal developers and users. You will be responsible for designing and developing highly scalable back-end and UI systems to navigate PayPal Login experience. You are self-motivated, have excellent design instincts, and enjoy working with multi-functional teams. We are looking for a talented Software Engineer with a passion for building great developer experiences and capable of thriving in a fast-paced and fun work environment.
