The Sr. Learning Platform Engineer will be responsible for developing and maintaining any code associated with integrations between Learning Platforms including but not limited to the learning management system (LMS), lab platforms, curriculum and lab publishing, and productivity tools. The Staff Learning Platform Engineer will build relationships with stakeholders within Learning Enablement (LE) to automate manual processes and ensure the most efficient and effective systems are available to create effective training for our customers. This role will collaborate with cross-functional stakeholders and external platform vendors, define business requirements, architect, create, and maintain the code base for any automation, and continuously seek to improve the user experience for any customers using said automation.

### The impact you will have:

  * Architect, develop, and maintain automation for the business to enable curriculum development and training delivery

  * Create API-based solutions to integrate solutions between learning platforms

  * Identify and solve manual processes to automate

  * Build relationships with the organization and adjacent business units to solve larger problems as a team

  * Lead vendor teams handling publishing for content, including localized content

### What we look for:

  * 4-year degree (or equivalent experience) in Computer Science or related field with demonstrated ability

  * 5+ years of experience in public cloud technologies (AWS, Google Cloud, or Azure)

  * Proven experience in CICD tools (Source Control, Build, Deploy)

  * ### Ability to solve high-complexity issues

  * Experience communicating updates and resolutions to customers and cross-functional stakeholders

  * Ability to take a given problem and complete a solution

  * Extensive development experience with JavaScript, CSS, Node.js, Python, and other API technologies

  * Experience in API design and creating RESTful API patterns

  * Understanding of learning management systems (LMS)

  * Deep knowledge of lab platform technologies
