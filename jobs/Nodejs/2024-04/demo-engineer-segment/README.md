As a Demo Engineer, you will have a large impact on the go-to-market execution at Segment. You will work alongside the Presales team to drive innovation into these demo environments to maximize outcomes for sales and partners. You will also act as a critical liaison between Sales and the Product team, sharing feedback with the Product Management, Operations, and Engineering functions at Segment.
