This is a **remote position.**

**Position Description:**

**Ardent** is seeking a highly skilled and motivated **Test Automation Engineer** to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in test automation, particularly for Esri software solutions. This role is integral to ensuring the quality and performance of our software applications through effective automation strategies.

**Key Responsibilities:**

  * **Test Automation Development:** Design, develop, and maintain automated test scripts and frameworks for Esri software products. Utilize tools and methodologies to ensure comprehensive test coverage and efficient execution.
  * **Test Planning:** Collaborate with project stakeholders to understand requirements and define test plans, including both functional and performance criteria for Esri applications.
  * **Test Execution:** Execute automated tests, analyze results, and document test outcomes. Identify and report defects, and work with development teams to facilitate timely resolution.
  * **Test Strategy:** Collaborate with product managers, developers, and QA engineers to define test strategies, create test plans, and determine appropriate test automation approaches for Esri software.
  * **Problem-Solving:** Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills with the ability to troubleshoot issues and propose effective solutions.
  * **Collaboration:** Strong communication and teamwork abilities, with experience working in a collaborative environment.
  * **Attention to Detail:** Meticulous attention to detail with a commitment to producing high-quality work.
  * **Test Reporting:** Analyze results, and provide detailed reports on test outcomes, including defect identification and resolution.
  * **Integration:** Work closely with development and QA teams to integrate automated testing into continuous integration/continuous deployment (CI/CD) pipelines.
  * **Documentation:** Maintain clear and comprehensive documentation of test processes, scripts, and results. Ensure that all documentation adheres to federal contracting standards.
  * **Issue Tracking:** Track and manage defect reports and test results, ensuring accurate logging and follow-up on issues discovered during automated testing.
  * **Issue Resolution:** Troubleshoot, and resolve issues related to test automation and software quality. Provide actionable feedback to development teams.
  * **Federal Contracting Compliance:** Ensure that all test automation activities comply with federal contracting requirements and standards.
