### About this role:

Our Telematics team takes real-time signals (think: GPS, Engine RPM, Fuel Levels and hundreds more) from our IoT devices deployed in vehicles and processes them to help our customers make their vehicle fleets safer, greener, and more efficient.

The Telematics firmware team is a cross-functional product engineering team that designs, develops, deploys, and maintains solutions that help our customers become more sustainable in their fleet operations. We primarily work and collaborate closely with product managers, customer support teams, other firmware teams, and full stack engineers to build out our feature sets. The technology in use includes buildkite, databricks, GraphQL, Golang, C++, Linux, CAN, Wifi, USB and LTE. This role will both develop new features and engage in operational ownership of those features as they are deployed to our fleet of devices.
