### **What You Will Be Doing**

Reporting to the Director of Customer Deployment, this position requires an Electrical Engineering Degree from an Accredited Academic Institution and a strong familiarity with the technical aspects of the components of a site including; balance of plant or system, electric vehicle supply equipment, and technology used in different types of field projects. The Site Engineer will be an excellent communicator and problem-solver and able to manage crises when they arise. The role requires frequent travel to various project sites, taking measurements, aggregating data, interpreting results, and designing solutions during any phase of the project lifecycle. Additionally, the Site Engineer will be required to instruct and manage qualified workers to perform similar tasks as needed.
