 **Position Overview**

The Digital Customer Experience (DXC) team's Customer Success domain is looking for a hands-on, experienced engineering manager to manage a team of experienced and driven engineers responsible for building, maintaining, and supporting Autodesk's next-generation knowledge platform. You will report into Senior Manager, Engineering. You will focus on leading an engineering team that develops and delivers reliable and scalable backend API services to support the frontend web experiences for our customers, and other consuming application providing new ways for them to interact with Autodesk. You will help lead the backend development of our platform, using Java, Node.js, Python, Docker and other web and DevOps technologies. We are looking for someone who understands complex requirements, back-office systems, and user experience flows, all while delivering a feature-rich platform to have direct impact on our customers.

  * Collaborate with a geographically dispersed team and stakeholders.

  * Hire and grow top engineering talent.

  * Provide insight, feedback, and recommendations to senior management.

  * Automate processes and propose new tools and technologies when needed.

  * Lead planning, execution, and success of technical projects through Agile ceremonies.

  * Perform code reviews, evaluate implementations, and provide feedback for tool improvements.

  * Work with Autodesk's engineering teams to ensure applications and services are highly available and reliable.

  * Engage in technical and architectural discussions; collaborate with stakeholders to understand requirements, understand use cases, and build toward a cohesive technical strategy.
