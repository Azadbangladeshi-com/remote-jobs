### What You Will Be Doing

The Manager, Global Quality will work cross-functionally to maintain and continuously improve the ChargePoint Quality Management System, Environmental Management System (EMS), Health and Safety Management (OHS) and Information Security Management (ISM) in compliance with ISO 9001 / 14001 / 45001/ 27001 globally. They will Proactively monitor the various elements of the QMS, EMS, OHS and ISM to help ensure compliance with quality and environmental system regulations and applicable national and international standards.  
  
 **Essential Duties And Responsibilities  
**

  * Provide support to internal functions in the application, maintenance and improvement of quality systems, procedures and department specific processes
  * Manager, lead and oversee a team in Quality Systems Engineering
  * Manage and conduct company-wide internal audits in preparation for external audits including planning, implementation, report generation, and corrective action issuance to closure
  * Coordinate external audits by the registrar or by customers
  * Prepare performance reports and metrics for the QMS, EMS, OHS and ISM; 
  * Ensure efficient and effective processes and tools are established for compliance with ISO standards requirements
  * Manage the CAPA process to including root cause analysis, corrective action and ongoing review to insure effectiveness
  * Conduct local and global training on key elements of the QMS, EMS and ISM, including CAPA, Complaint and Internal Audit
  * Execute projects and complete other duties as assigned
