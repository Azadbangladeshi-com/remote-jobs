 **Job Summary**

We are looking for a Software Engineer for the Beeswax Data Platform Engineering team. Beeswax is the demand side platform of FreeWheel, helping advertisers and buyers transact with the programmatic ecosystem. Our team mainly focuses on building a dependable data platform and delivering precise data to the customers in a cost-efficient manner. The Beeswax Data Platform processes billions of ad events daily, delivers real-time and batch logs to the customers, and provides various reporting and troubleshooting products. As a software engineer, you will play a crucial role in the development, deployment, and maintenance of data pipelines and infrastructure within our organization. You will collaborate with cross-functional teams to design, implement, and optimize data solutions that enable efficient data processing, storage, and analysis.
