**Quick Take:** Axios is a growth-stage company dedicated to providing trustworthy, award-winning news content in an audience-first format. We're hiring a Data Analyst for our data platform team!

 **Why it Matters:** Our data analyst will champion a data-informed culture by empowering others to use data to make responsible decisions, ask smart questions, and by performing complex ad-hoc analyses for key stakeholders. This person will work with a cross-functional team to provide data products and insights that help power Axios.

**The Details:** You must be a highly collaborative problem solver and capable of understanding how data is implemented, distributed, and consumed. An understanding of how to work with large amounts of data to support or refute a hypothesis is also essential in this role. A strong candidate should be able to analyze, translate, and communicate effectively about data with product and engineering teams as well as other key stakeholders. The ideal candidate will have a passion for Axios, a collaborative spirit, and possess the following skills:

  * Strong analytical skills and the ability to analyze and disseminate significant amounts of information with attention to detail and accuracy
  * Applied experience and strong proficiency with SQL 
  * Demonstrated experience working with large data sets and relational databases (Postgres, MySQL, Redshift, BigQuery etc)
  * Familiarity with data modeling, common data warehouse schemas (Star, Snowflake), and data transformational tools such as DBT 
  * Ability to analyze large datasets to identify patterns/trends and apply statistical techniques
  * Experience with using business intelligence tools (Looker preferred, but not required)
  * Experience and comfort with data analysis and reporting for business stakeholders, external clients or product and technology teams
  * Strong growth and curiosity-driven mindset, with an interest in gaining experience working with a variety of stakeholders, translating business requirements, and presenting findings
  * Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  * A strong track record of collaborating effectively with colleagues such as product managers or data engineers
  * Ability to accurately estimate the complexity of projects
  * Python proficiency is preferred, but not required
