## Job Description:

Intel Labs is an industry-leading research organization, responsible for driving Intel's technology pipeline, product innovations, and creating new opportunities. The mission of Intel Labs is to deliver breakthrough technologies to fuel Intel's growth. This includes identifying and exploring emerging technologies and high-risk opportunities ahead of business unit investment and demonstrating first-to-market technologies and innovative new usages for computing technology. Intel Labs engages the leading thinkers in academia, open source, and industry in addition to partnering closely with Intel business units.  
  
This internship position is with the Security and Privacy Research group in Intel Labs. Our mission is to drive industry-leading research on Cloud Security, Confidential Computing, Secure AI/ML, Cryptography, and Privacy to deliver higher robustness and assurance leveraging Intel platforms. As an intern in this position, you will join the Trustworthy Distributed Systems team. In our work, we develop and evaluate how new software and hardware-assisted techniques can enable novel approaches to establish the trustworthiness of code, data, and infrastructure in highly distributed computing environments.

During your internship, you will focus on designing and developing novel approaches to addressing some of the most pressing security, privacy, and trust problems in the area of software supply chain. You will have the opportunity to explore algorithmic- and hardware-based techniques running on the latest software and system architectures for cloud computing. You will develop prototypes to evaluate and enhance your research ideas. You will have the opportunity to display your work through demos, whitepapers, and presentations at internal and external forums. Your work will help in laying the foundation for future Intel products.

**The ideal candidate should exhibit the following behavioral traits:**

  * Good analytical and problem-solving skills with the ability to multitask.

  * Ability to work in a collaborative and team-oriented environment with effective communication.

  * Self-motivated learner.

  * Effective communication skills as demonstrated by published papers and talks.

  * Agility to quickly adapt to changing requirements that arise due to new research results or customer needs.
