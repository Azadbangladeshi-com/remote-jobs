**About The Role**

As a Senior Data Analyst you will be working with both on chain and off chain data to help us measure and understand our data feeds products. You will need to utilize your skills with data tools in order to create metrics and dashboards that help our product teams make integral decisions to mitigate risk.

  

#### Your Impact

    * Take ownership of generating risk mitigation plans to accelerate our growth.
    * Help define how future products will mitigate risk.
    * Develop and maintain metrics and dashboards to guide and drive business decisions.
    * Partner with cross functional teams to provide recommendations on how best to mitigate risk for some of our most critical data products.

#### Requirements

    * A strong understanding of equity, forex, and/or crypto markets
    * Strong SQL skills
    * Experience working with risk mitigation
    * Ability to take ownership in building metrics and dashboards
    * Understanding of how data can be used to guide and drive decisions
    * Deep understanding of statistics

#### Preferred Requirements

    * Experience in the blockchain Defi Space and working with blockchain data
    * Experience with Python for statistical analysis and ETL
    * Deep focus on continuous learning
    * Strong independent thinker
    * Experience with optimization and modeling problems
    * Solid experience with GCP Data tools
