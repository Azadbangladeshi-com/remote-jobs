###  **Job Description**

Scalable Capital was built in the cloud from day one. Our services currently run on various AWS services like ECS, Fargate and Lambda and utilise a multi account strategy.  
We embrace a DevOps culture where the development teams manage their own CI/CD pipelines and cloud infrastructure for their services themselves. Our Site Reliability Engineering Team focuses on a reusable and modular infrastructure to enable the development teams to deploy and operate services in the cloud productively and securely.

  * You will continuously improve our Cloud Foundation in AWS
  * You will be responsible for improving infrastructure and deployment automation using tools like Terraform, AWS Lambda and Python
  * You will develop custom tools and scripts to automate on AWS
  * You will mentor and enable our engineering teams to further foster our DevOps culture

  * A passion for automating and improving processes

  * Previous experience in networking, audit-logging, and access tracing is an advantage
