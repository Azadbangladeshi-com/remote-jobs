## **The Opportunity**

Flock Safety is looking for the first Audio Quality Assurance Engineer. In this role, you will be instrumental in how we improve, build, and evaluate the audio quality of our current and future audio and camera products. The Audio Engineering team is responsible for understanding the audio challenges across multiple landscapes, and is responsible for ensuring excellent and consistent audio performance across our line of products. You will work across multiple teams to ensure both hardware and software audio quality with repeatable, high-quality audio measurements.

**This position is on-site in Tampere, Finland.**

## **The Skillset**

  * Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Engineering, Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, or a related field with at least 4 years of experience
  * Experience in hardware and software audio analysis, tooling, and testing
  * Knowledge and experience in managing audio laboratories
  * Proficiency in carrying out professional audio measurements
  * Understanding of acoustics and sound propagation principles
  * Knowledge of audio design principles and techniques
  * Proficiency in Python for test automation and audio test tool development
  * Experience with source control solutions such as Git
  * Ability to work effectively across multiple projects and teams
  * Strong verbal and written communication skills
