###  **Job Description**

The Engineering Experience team at NBCU is defining and building a paved road of tools and systems to accelerate and optimize our software development teams and practices. As a member of our Cloud Enablement & Acceleration FinOps team, you will design, implement, and manage robust data pipelines to develop dashboards that deliver actionable insights into our infrastructure costs and utilization patterns. You will collaborate within our team, and with other stakeholders, integrating essential business metrics with detailed cost data to drive financial accountability and technological advancement. If you have a passion for working with Product Owners and Engineering teams to build high-quality, production-grade data pipelines, ELT processes, and dashboards that will inform strategic and operational decisions, we'd love to hear from you.

As part of the larger Engineering Experience team, you will be a part of building world class systems to improve development workflows and productivity and help us create world class tools and experiences.

Our organization values collaboration and continuous improvement, and we strive to provide an environment where all ideas are valued.  **Basic Requirements:**

  * Bachelor's degree or equivalent education, experience, and training in Computer Science, Data Science or a related field
  * 5+ years' experience as part of a team building reporting, business intelligence, and analytics solutions
  * Applied experience with Public Cloud Governance and FinOps best practices
  * Applied experience with Snowflake, BigQuery, Tableau, Looker, or QuickSight
  * Applied experience analyzing cost and usage patterns of various systems that span across multiple clouds and data centers
