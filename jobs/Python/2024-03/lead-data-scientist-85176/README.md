 **Job Description:**

 **Your way to impact**

  * You believe in data-driven decisions and use data to create risk management strategies and answer business questions. 
  * You are hyper-analytical, intellectually honest, and extremely passionate about data and testing (e.g., A/B experimentation). 
  * You are a highly motivated, result-oriented, enjoy working in a fast-paced environment, and can deliver successful results with required guidance. 

**Your day to day**

  * Lead the development, refinement, and execution of strategies to mitigate credit risk in the commercial lending portfolio.
  * Take ownership of Targeting , Pricing and Line Management key performance indicators (KPIs) and metrics related to credit risk for new accounts.
  * Identify new risk management opportunities and assess creditworthiness of businesses through deep dive analysis leveraging various data sources including credit bureau, bank statements and financial statement data etc.
  * Utilize large datasets to conduct in-depth analyses to optimize Line and Pricing decisions.
  * Collaborate with other teams including Marketing, Product, Platform for performance tracking and root cause analysis.
  * Lead end-to-end project execution with minimal assistance as a credit risk expert.
  * Translate ambiguous, unstructured business problems into actionable data-driven analyses.
  * Effectively communicate complex findings and strategy recommendations to both technical and non-technical audiences.
  * Support multiple projects at the same time in a fast-paced, results-oriented environment 
  * Keep up-to-date with industry trends, best practices, and emerging risks related to commercial lending.
