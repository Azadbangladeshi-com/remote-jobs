### **About Dimagi**

Dimagi is an award-winning social enterprise and a certified B Corp and Benefit Corporation. Our primary product, CommCare, is the most widely-deployed open-source digital platform for enabling Frontline Workers. Since 2002, Governments and organizations across all sectors have built customized mobile, web, and SMS applications on CommCare to deliver services to underserved populations. Dimagi is more than a tech company. We are an impact-first team of compassionate people from all parts of the world and all walks of life, dedicated to using business as a force for good in global health and development.

###  **About the Position:**

Dimagi is seeking a Junior Software Engineering Manager with a team and project management background to join our tech team in South Africa. This position is open to remote employment within South Africa or can be based out of our office in Cape Town. You will collaborate with team members in the US and may require some of your working hours to overlap with EST working hours.

The work will be challenging, but in the right ways - you'll have an opportunity to quickly dive into and learn from multiple project areas while making a real, positive impact. We are looking for a reliable team member who excels in execution and bringing a team together. The Associate Engineering Manager will be responsible for project management guidance, people management, and mentoring to improve the team's effectiveness.

###  **Responsibilities:**

As an Associate Engineering Manager, you will:

  * Manage a team of engineers and technical staff to enable their potential and help shape the growth of the team while also ensuring timely execution of team deliverables and helping to guide their resourcing
  * Work with our Director of Technology to help understand the priority of technical asks and drive the engineering roadmap for the assigned areas
  * Ensure timely execution of team deliverables and help to guide their resourcing
  * Perform Scrum Master role to manage agile sprints and work with team to identify and improve processes continuously 
  * Lead management of our third-party development partners to implement project features, guiding the technical process and working with the project team from scoping to delivery
  * Lead execution of internal team initiatives around process improvement and team planning

  * Project management skills with demonstrated experience leading multi-person technical projects
  * Planning and organizational skills that result in successful implementation and follow-through
  * Ability to thrive in a fast-paced, dynamic environment and adapt to changing priorities
