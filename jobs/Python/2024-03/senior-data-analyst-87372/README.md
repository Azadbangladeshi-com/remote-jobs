A Senior Data Analyst is pivotal in leading the interpretation and analysis of complex datasets and driving strategic decisions through advanced analytics. This role sets the direction for data-driven strategies, works closely with leadership, and mentors junior analysts. The Senior Data Analyst is influential in guiding cross-functional teams to implement data-driven solutions.

**Key Responsibilities**

  * Should have expertise in analysing large datasets, identifying business insights from data, and summarising their analysis into reports and presentations.
  * Experience in working with business stakeholders.
  * Spot, analyse, and communicate high-level trends or patterns within intricate datasets
  * Collaborate closely with senior management to set and align data-driven strategies with core business objectives
  * Creation of in-depth visualisation reports and present to the leadership team for strategic decision-making
  * Champion the identification and implementation of advanced process improvements using data
  * Guide cross-functional teams in the development of sophisticated data solutions with a broader impact on business strategies
  * Offer senior-level consulting, analytics, and recommendations to Marketing leadership
  * Provide expert-level technical support and mentor junior analysts within the team

**Qualifications & Competencies**

  * Minimum 6 years of experience in data roles, preferably with diverse environments
  * Qualification - B.E./B.Tech/MCA/MS/Other Graduation
  * Excellent stakeholder management skills and a go-getter attitude 
  * Exceptional interpersonal, presentation, and communication skills.
  * Experience working with geographically distributed teams and complex projects. 
  * Highly analytical with strong problem-solving skills
  * **Expertise in SQL or Python,** MS-Excel, and other data tools.
