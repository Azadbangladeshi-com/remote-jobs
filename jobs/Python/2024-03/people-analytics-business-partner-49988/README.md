### Job Description

As an Analytics Business Partner on the People Analytics & Research team, you will drive the integration of data insights into strategic decisions, working closely with leaders to enhance the People team's impact. You have a strong background in quantitative analysis, experience translating complex data into actionable insights, and a proven track record of effective client or stakeholder relationship management. Your ability to collaborate with People Research Scientists, prioritize varied projects, and influence leaders with evidence-based advice will be crucial for driving organizational growth and critical People decisions throughout Block.

### You will:

  * Collaborate with People team leaders and cross-functional partners to provide evidence-based insights and recommendations that support their strategic goals.
  * Conduct research with People Research Scientists to address strategic questions and challenges.
  * Prioritize strategic goals and guide client teams on leveraging people data insights to achieve critical business outcomes.
  * Use qualitative and quantitative analysis to formulate recommendations and drive robust, evidence-based People decisions.
  * Respond to complex inbound inquiries with resource guidance, insights, and recommendations.
  * Work on cyclical and special projects supporting People team priorities, ensuring data accuracy and relevance.

  * Act as a liaison between the business unit and People Analytics & Research team, ensuring seamless communication and project execution.
