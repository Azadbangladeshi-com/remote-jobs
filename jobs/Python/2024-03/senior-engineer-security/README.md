 **What you will do (or learn to do)**

  * Design, build, and launch robust security solutions that enhance the security posture of Eightfold's production infrastructure, ensuring a secure environment for both Eightfold applications and data.
  * Act as a technical authority on security, providing expertise and guidance on security topics to internal teams, prospects, and customers.
  * Collaborate with prospects and customers on security assessments, ensuring their security concerns are addressed.
  * Engage with auditors to demonstrate the security measures in place and work on continuous improvement of the infrastructure security.
  * Partner with product engineering teams to foster a culture of security awareness and ensure the secure development of software across the board.

  

 **What you should already know**

  * Solid engineering fundamentals with a hands-on approach to tackling issues.
  * 3-8 years of experience writing secure and scalable code.
  * Comprehensive systems knowledge encompassing networking, compute, and storage concepts.
  * Proficiency in cloud technologies such as AWS, and experience building SaaS applications.
  * Passion for security topics and understanding how it can drive business growth.
  * Excellent communication and interpersonal skills for effective collaboration.
  * Familiarity with recognized security frameworks and standards such as NIST, CIS benchmarks, and DISA STIG.

  

 **Good to have**

  * Certifications like OSCP, or equivalent are beneficial but not mandatory.
  * Experience with security monitoring tools, incident response protocols, and forensic analysis.
  * Knowledge of threat modeling and risk assessment techniques.
  * Previous experience in conducting internal and external penetration testing.
  * Familiarity with legal and regulatory compliance including GDPR, HIPAA, SOC 2, or similar.
  * Active involvement in the security community, such as publications, open-source projects, or speaking engagements at security conferences.
