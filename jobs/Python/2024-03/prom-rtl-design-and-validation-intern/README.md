### Job Description:

You will be part of Intel Advanced Design Organization (AD) within Design Enablement (DE) focused on pathfinding and development of advanced memory technology and circuits to enable best-in-class memory collateral/IP and product design across all generations of Intel process technology.

You'll be involved in deep sub-micron VLSI design activities in Intel's leading process technologies, developing competitive solutions with Intel's PROM circuit technology for use in mobile, IOT, client, network and server segments.

Job responsibilities include mixed signal design, validation, automation and documentation activities for PROM IP designs as needed.

This is a remote internship for 6+ months.
