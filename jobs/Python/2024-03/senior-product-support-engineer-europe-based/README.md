### Job Summary

We are looking for a Senior Product Support Engineer to join our enterprise product support organization. You will work with customer solutions, engineering, product managers, and various other stakeholders to deliver consistent software product experience for our customers. You will use your skills in Application and Systems troubleshooting, customer support, communication and experience working with enterprise customers to deliver great customer experiences.

### Core Responsibilities

  * Deliver expert technical product support to our enterprise customers using the SandboxAQ Security Suite while providing exceptional customer experiences.
  * Troubleshoot and resolve technical issues with the deployment and operation of the Security Suite web interface, APIs and related tracers and scanners using troubleshooting tools and resources such as log files, documentation and application code.
  * Work closely with engineering and product teams to communicate product issues and contribute to product and process improvements.
  * Leverage your ability to read and understand application code to troubleshoot and Identify root cause of customer reported issues.
