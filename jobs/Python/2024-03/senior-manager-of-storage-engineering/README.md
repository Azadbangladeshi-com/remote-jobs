**Position Summary**

GEICO is in search of a Senior Manager for Enterprise Storage Engineering to lead our data center and private cloud storage engineering initiatives. This visionary will lead our storage engineering team and strategy, focusing on innovative development, implementation, and enhancement of traditional and SDS solutions within our data centers and private cloud environments. The role is central to ensuring our storage infrastructure is robust and adaptive, meeting current needs and future technological advancements. This leader will be pivotal in driving efficiency, security, and scalability across our storage platforms, reinforcing GEICO's strategic edge in the industry.

 **Position Description**

As the Senior Manager for Data Center Storage Engineering, you will lead the strategic development, optimization, and integration of advanced storage methodologies, including object, file, and block storage, as well as SAN technologies, within various service paradigms such as bare metal, Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS), and Platform as a Service (PaaS). You'll provide visionary leadership and strategic direction, ensuring the team aligns with the company's business goals and objectives. Your role will involve close collaboration with senior leadership, IT teams, and external partners to drive innovation in our storage capabilities.
