The Systems Engineer is part of the Infrastructure Engineering team at Granicus. The team supports and maintains infrastructure hardware, software, and critical systems with a focus on security, availability, and scalability. The ideal candidate is an excellent Linux system administrator with significant enterprise experience designing and supporting a Linux-based infrastructure in a multi-cloud environment.

### What You'll Do:

  * Follow infrastructure team processes and actively seek to improve them, especially as it relates to implementing automation of repeatable tasks 
  * Identify and fix problem areas in the existing Linux OS and DNS infrastructure 
  * Execute quality changes, perform code and change request reviews 
  * As part of an on-call team, triage and troubleshoot efficiently and effectively during production incidents 
  * Ensure system resources and assets are logged and monitored along with appropriate actions for events 
  * Analyze log files and other infrastructure data using available tools 
  * Develop documentation to empower other team members and accurately represent projects and procedures 
  * Plan for and install new software/firmware releases and hardware/software upgrades to meet security and supportability requirements 
  * Act as a point of escalation for other Linux Systems Engineers 
  * Manage regular patching and other activities to address security vulnerabilities 

### Who You Are:

  * You have 5+ years of relevant experience in Linux system administration 
  * You have 3+ years of experience scripting / and automating repeatable tasks (e.g., Bash, Python, Ansible, Terraform, Git, PowerShell) 
  * You have 3+ years of relevant experience in DNS administration 
  * You have specific technical experience with: 
  * automated updating/patching of Linux-based systems 
  * SSL certificates and certificate management 
  * 2 or more different Linux distributions 
  * You have experience working to comply with security frameworks such as ISO 27001, NIST 800-53 and/or FedRAMP 
  * You have worked in a large enterprise environment, providing Linux and DNS support 
  * You are passionate about technology and keep abreast of technical developments in your field 
  * You are a motivated self-starter capable of picking up work on your own 
  * You are a proven technical leader, capable of identifying problems and then designing, and implementing solutions 

### Specific System Experience:

  * The ideal candidate has some level of experience with the following infrastructure technologies (bonus if your experience matches the specific products at Granicus in parenthesis): 
  * Linux OS (RHEL, Amazon Linux, Ubuntu, CentOS) 
  * Container systems (Docker, Kubernetes, EKS) 
  * DNS and IP Management (Infoblox) 
  * System monitoring tools (ELK, LogicMonitor) 
  * Virtualization (VMware) 
  * Cloud computing (AWS,Azure) 
  * Server hardware (Cisco UCS)
