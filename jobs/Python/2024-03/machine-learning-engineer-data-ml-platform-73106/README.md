### Job Description

All of Block's product services are organized into business units with their own product and engineering teams. Underneath, there's a connected platform team that supports the company. We are that platform. Within the platform, our team's focus is on developer productivity in Data and AI. We bring tools that enable engineers to orchestrate workflows, train models, easily onboard onto foundational infrastructures, and improve their daily development experience. Our team works cross-functionally across Block's business units to deliver the most impactful tools to developers, and oftentimes working with teams to understand specific needs, and then generalizing those across Block.

We're looking for an enthusiastic and experienced Machine Learning Engineer to help us use AI to build impactful developer productivity tools for engineers across Block. You'll bring together content from across Block's business units and build a foundational connective layer to power AI applications to increase developer productivity.
