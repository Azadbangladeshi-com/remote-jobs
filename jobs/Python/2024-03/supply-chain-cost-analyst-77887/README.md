### About the role:

Samsara is seeking an experienced Supply Chain Cost Analyst responsible for hardware spend budget management. In this role, you will partner with Hardware Engineering, Supply Chain, Manufacturing, Planning, and Finance to forecast, track, and report non-material spend across the hardware product lifecycle from development and sustainment through end of life. The Supply Chain Cost Analyst collaborates with cross-functional teams to forecast spend across various non-material spend categories inclusive of capital equipment, tooling, fixtures, certification, and prototypes.
