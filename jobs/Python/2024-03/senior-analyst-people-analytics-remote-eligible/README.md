### Minimum Qualifications:

  * Minimum 5+ years of combined experience across Business Intelligence, Data visualization, and/or People Analytics.
  * Extensive experience in designing, developing, and managing Tableau dashboards.
  * Strong experience with SQL (relational databases, data validation, writing efficient code for big data).
  * Programming language (Python, R) is a plus.
  * Excellent business acumen and an ability to lead with data. Can combine qualitative findings with data analytics to make comprehensive recommendations.
