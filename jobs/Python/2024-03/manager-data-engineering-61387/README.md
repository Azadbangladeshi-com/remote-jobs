### The Role:

SoFi is seeking an experienced and motivated Data Engineering Manager to lead a part of our Data Engineering function that supports business units across all of SoFi and will be responsible for leading a strategy for building a self-serve and scalable analytics platform. The mission of the Data Engineering team at SoFi is to activate data throughout SoFi, enabling the creation of personalized and delightful experiences for our members. The Data Engineering team is responsible for providing our Users a seamless experience to leverage data to make data driven business decisions for all of SoFi.

As the single-threaded leader for this group, you will establish the vision and strategy to build foundational and critical data products for Lending business units, which are highly leveraged across SoFi for analytical, reporting, and machine learning use-cases. You will also be responsible for driving self-serve and scalable analytics initiatives that make users lives easier to access data with high integrity and quality. The Data Products Group establishes and promotes best practices for data management and governance across SoFi.

We are currently seeking an experienced and highly motivated Data Engineering Manager to lead our Data Products group. In this role, your primary responsibilities will include developing and executing the data engineering strategy, driving data architecture initiatives, and overseeing the design and implementation of scalable data products. Collaboration with cross-functional teams will be essential to ensure data quality, integrity, and availability while promoting best practices for data management. This leadership position demands a strong technical background in data engineering, exceptional management skills, and the ability to thrive in a fast-paced, results-oriented environment. As a leader of leaders, you will play a crucial role in guiding and mentoring your team, fostering their professional growth, and ensuring they have the necessary resources and support to effectively lead their respective teams.
